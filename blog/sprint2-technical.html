whats the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

Margin

Margin is the space between the border and the other elements you are working with in your project. The margin goes around all four sides of the element, but you're able to adjust it for any given side you choose.

Padding

Padding is the space thatâ€™s inside the element between the element and the border. Padding goes around all four sides of the content and you can target and change the padding for each side similar to the margin.

Border

The border is the area inbetween the margin and the padding. Just like the previous two you can adjust the sides of the border to be exactly hwo you wish. You can also use borders change the shape of an element. for insstance you are able to turn a square picture into a circle by playing with the border radius.
